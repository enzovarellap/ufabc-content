1 · Derivadas

▶1a\(y=\operatorname{sen}(\cos x)\)
1
Regra da cadeia: derivada de fora \(\operatorname{sen}'(\,\cdot\,)=\cos(\,\cdot\,)\), vezes a derivada de dentro \((\cos x)'=-\operatorname{sen} x\).
Resposta
\(\;y'=-\operatorname{sen}(x)\,\cos(\cos x)\)✓ confere com o gabarito
▶1b\(y=e^{\operatorname{tg} x}\)
1
Cadeia: \((e^{u})'=e^{u}u'\) com \(u=\operatorname{tg} x\) e \(u'=\sec^2 x\).
Resposta
\(\;y'=e^{\operatorname{tg} x}\,\sec^2 x\)✓ confere com o gabarito
▶1c\(y=\dfrac{e^{x}}{\operatorname{sen}(3x)+2x\cos x}\)
1
Regra do quociente \(\left(\frac{u}{v}\right)'=\frac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}\), com \(u=e^x\ (u'=e^x)\) e \(v=\operatorname{sen}3x+2x\cos x\).
2
\(v'=3\cos 3x+\underbrace{(2\cos x-2x\operatorname{sen} x)}_{(2x\cos x)'}=3\cos 3x+2\cos x-2x\operatorname{sen} x\).
Resposta
\(\;y'=\dfrac{e^{x}\big[(\operatorname{sen}3x+2x\cos x)-(3\cos 3x+2\cos x-2x\operatorname{sen} x)\big]}{(\operatorname{sen}3x+2x\cos x)^2}\)✓ confere com o gabarito (o "\(\ln 2\)" que aparece no gabarito impresso é um erro de digitação — o termo certo é \(2\cos x\))

7 · Integrais por substituição

Ideia
Escolha \(u=\) "a parte interna"; calcule \(du\); troque tudo para \(u\); integre; volte para \(x\).
▶7a\(\displaystyle\int \operatorname{sen}(3x)\,dx\)
1
\(u=3x\Rightarrow du=3\,dx\Rightarrow dx=\tfrac{du}{3}\).
2
\(\int \operatorname{sen} u\,\tfrac{du}{3}=-\tfrac13\cos u\).
Resposta
\(\;-\tfrac13\cos(3x)+C\)✓ gabarito
▶7b\(\displaystyle\int x(4+x^2)^{10}\,dx\)
1
\(u=4+x^2\Rightarrow du=2x\,dx\Rightarrow x\,dx=\tfrac{du}{2}\).
2
\(\int u^{10}\,\tfrac{du}{2}=\tfrac{1}{2}\cdot\tfrac{u^{11}}{11}=\tfrac{u^{11}}{22}\).
Resposta
\(\;\tfrac{1}{22}(4+x^2)^{11}+C\)✓ gabarito
▶7c\(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{\operatorname{sen}\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx\)
1
\(u=\sqrt{x}\Rightarrow du=\tfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\,dx\Rightarrow \tfrac{dx}{\sqrt{x}}=2\,du\).
2
\(\int \operatorname{sen} u\cdot 2\,du=-2\cos u\).
Resposta
\(\;-2\cos\sqrt{x}+C\)✓ gabarito
▶7d\(\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi/2} e^{\operatorname{sen}\theta}\cos\theta\,d\theta\)
1
\(u=\operatorname{sen}\theta\Rightarrow du=\cos\theta\,d\theta\). Limites: \(\theta=0\Rightarrow u=0\); \(\theta=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\Rightarrow u=1\).
2
\(\int_0^1 e^{u}\,du=\big[e^{u}\big]_0^1=e-1\).
Resposta
\(\;e-1\)✓ gabarito

8 · Integração por partes

Fórmula
\(\displaystyle\int u\,dv=uv-\int v\,du\). Escolha \(u\) (o que simplifica ao derivar — costuma ser o polinômio) e \(dv\) o resto.
▶8a\(\displaystyle\int x\,e^{2x}\,dx\)
1
\(u=x\ (du=dx)\), \(dv=e^{2x}dx\ (v=\tfrac12 e^{2x})\).
2
\(=\tfrac{x}{2}e^{2x}-\int \tfrac12 e^{2x}dx=\tfrac{x}{2}e^{2x}-\tfrac14 e^{2x}\).
Resposta
\(\;\tfrac14 e^{2x}(2x-1)+C\)✓ gabarito
▶8b\(\displaystyle\int x\,\operatorname{sen}(3x)\,dx\)
1
\(u=x\ (du=dx)\), \(dv=\operatorname{sen}3x\,dx\ (v=-\tfrac13\cos 3x)\).
2
\(=-\tfrac{x}{3}\cos 3x+\int \tfrac13\cos 3x\,dx=-\tfrac{x}{3}\cos 3x+\tfrac19\operatorname{sen}3x\).
Resposta
\(\;\tfrac19\operatorname{sen}(3x)-\tfrac13 x\cos(3x)+C\)✓ gabarito
▶8c\(\displaystyle\int e^{x}\cos x\,dx\) (truque do "volta a integral")
1
Seja \(I=\int e^x\cos x\,dx\). Partes com \(u=e^x,\ dv=\cos x\,dx\): \(I=e^x\operatorname{sen} x-\int e^x\operatorname{sen} x\,dx\).
2
Partes de novo em \(\int e^x\operatorname{sen} x\,dx=-e^x\cos x+\int e^x\cos x\,dx=-e^x\cos x+I\).
3
Logo \(I=e^x\operatorname{sen} x-(-e^x\cos x+I)=e^x(\operatorname{sen} x+\cos x)-I\Rightarrow 2I=e^x(\operatorname{sen} x+\cos x)\).
Resposta
\(\;\tfrac12 e^{x}(\operatorname{sen} x+\cos x)+C\)✓ gabarito

9 · Frações parciais

Ideia
Fatore o denominador e quebre em frações simples \(\tfrac{A}{x-a}+\tfrac{B}{x-b}+\dots\). Se o grau do numerador \(\ge\) o do denominador, divida primeiro.
▶9a\(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{1}{x^2-16}\,dx\)
1
\(x^2-16=(x-4)(x+4)\). Decomponha: \(\dfrac{1}{(x-4)(x+4)}=\dfrac{1/8}{x-4}-\dfrac{1/8}{x+4}\).
2
\(\int=\tfrac18\ln|x-4|-\tfrac18\ln|x+4|\).
Resposta
\(\;\tfrac18\ln\left|\dfrac{x-4}{x+4}\right|+C\)✓ gabarito
▶9b\(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x}{x^2-5x+6}\,dx\)
1
\(x^2-5x+6=(x-2)(x-3)\). Ponha \(\dfrac{x}{(x-2)(x-3)}=\dfrac{A}{x-2}+\dfrac{B}{x-3}\).
2
\(x=A(x-3)+B(x-2)\). Em \(x=2\): \(2=-A\Rightarrow A=-2\). Em \(x=3\): \(3=B\).
Resposta
\(\;-2\ln|x-2|+3\ln|x-3|+C\)✓ gabarito
▶9c\(\displaystyle\int_3^4 \dfrac{7x+3}{(x-2)^2}\,dx\)
1
\(u=x-2\Rightarrow 7x+3=7u+17\). Integrando: \(\dfrac{7u+17}{u^2}=\dfrac{7}{u}+\dfrac{17}{u^2}\).
2
Primitiva: \(7\ln|u|-\dfrac{17}{u}=7\ln|x-2|-\dfrac{17}{x-2}\).
3
Em \(3\to4\): \(\big(7\ln2-\tfrac{17}{2}\big)-\big(7\ln1-17\big)=7\ln2-\tfrac{17}{2}+17\).
Resposta
\(\;\dfrac{17}{2}+7\ln 2\)✓ gabarito
▶9e\(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^2+2}{x^2-9}\,dx\)
1
Grau igual ⇒ divida: \(\dfrac{x^2+2}{x^2-9}=1+\dfrac{11}{x^2-9}\) (pois \(x^2+2=(x^2-9)+11\)).
2
\(\dfrac{11}{(x-3)(x+3)}=\dfrac{11}{6}\!\left(\dfrac{1}{x-3}-\dfrac{1}{x+3}\right)\).
3
\(\int=x+\tfrac{11}{6}\big(\ln|x-3|-\ln|x+3|\big)\).
Resposta
\(\;x+\tfrac{11}{6}\ln\left|\dfrac{x-3}{x+3}\right|+C\)✓ gabarito
▶9f\(\displaystyle\int \dfrac{x^4+x+1}{x^3-x}\,dx\)
1
Grau do topo maior ⇒ divida: \(\dfrac{x^4+x+1}{x^3-x}=x+\dfrac{x^2+x+1}{x^3-x}\) (resto após \( (x^3-x)\cdot x=x^4-x^2\)).
2
\(x^3-x=x(x-1)(x+1)\). \(\dfrac{x^2+x+1}{x(x-1)(x+1)}=\dfrac{-1}{x}+\dfrac{3/2}{x-1}+\dfrac{1/2}{x+1}\).
3
(Cobrindo: \(x=0\Rightarrow A=-1\); \(x=1\Rightarrow B=\tfrac32\); \(x=-1\Rightarrow C=\tfrac12\).)
Resposta
\(\;\dfrac{x^2}{2}-\ln|x|+\tfrac32\ln|x-1|+\tfrac12\ln|x+1|+C\)✓ gabarito
